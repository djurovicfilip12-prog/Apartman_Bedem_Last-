About This Apartment

In the quiet and most beautiful part of Nikšić, nestled below the ancient city fortress Bedem, just a five-minute walk from the vibrant city centre, sits Apartman Bedem I.

This spacious 70 m² apartment has been completely renovated and furnished to a high standard, offering the perfect combination of comfort and character. Every detail has been carefully considered to make your stay feel like home.

Whether you're travelling as a family, a group of friends, or on an extended business trip, Bedem I provides the space and amenities to make your stay exceptional.

✦ The apartment sleeps up to 7 guests, features air-conditioning, and offers two private terraces — one with views of the 4th-century Bedem Fortress, the other overlooking the Trebjesa forest.
